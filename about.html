Hunter McDaniel

Hunter McDaniel is a graduate student at the University of Florida located in Gainesville, Fl. She is currently studying to earn a master’s degree in web design and online communication. Hunter graduated with her bachelor’s degree in Advertising in the summer of 2018.

Hunter has experience working with the Adobe Creative Suite especially in InDesign and PhotoShop. Although she is most familiar with InDesign and Photoshop, she is working to learn more about Illustrator, Light Room and Premiere Pro.

Hunter is currently working at the University of Florida libraries while she is finishing her studies. After finishing her master’s degree, Hunter plans to move out of Gainesville, Fl to begin a career in online communications and web design.
